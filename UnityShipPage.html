Dynamic Ocean

Unity Engine


Inspired by Assassins Creed: Black Flag, this is my attempt at recreating the ocean and ship physics used in the game.

I started out with creating a basic trangle based mesh in Unity. The mesh is scalable.

Once the mesh was working properly I started experimenting with some materials.

I then modeled a low-poly boat in Blender and imported it into the scene.
