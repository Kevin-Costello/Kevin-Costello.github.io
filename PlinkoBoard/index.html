Plinko Board

Three JS


One of my projects working with the Three JS library.

You can left-click on the scene to drop a plinko ball at a random spot along the top of the board. While a little wonky on the browser, you can also rotate the camera by left-click-and-dragging, pan by right-click-and-draggin, and zoom by middle-click-and-dragging. You might need to click on the scene again to see this update. If your board fills up too much you can press 'Q' to remove all the balls from the scene.
